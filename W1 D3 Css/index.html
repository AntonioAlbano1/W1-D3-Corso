Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 once brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Methods

1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixrer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
